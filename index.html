What Sets Us Apart

THE PROCESS

There are Mexican restaurants. And then there's Casa Maíz. The difference is in what you don't see: the corn that arrived from Oaxaca, the lime that transformed it, the hands that pressed it.

01
🌽
Heirloom Corn

We import native varieties from Oaxaca and Guerrero directly from indigenous farmers. No industrial corn.

02
💧
Nixtamalization

We cook the corn in lime water (nixtamal) every dawn. This ancient process unlocks nutrients and creates real masa.

03
👐
Hand-Pressed

Every tortilla is hand-pressed on the tortilla press. We never use masa harina, never buy pre-made tortillas.

04
🔥
Wood Fire

All protein is cooked over live fire. Natural smoke flavor you can't replicate with gas or a convection oven.

Our Story

FROM OAXACA
TO DUBAI.

It started with a comal, a sack of dried corn, and a recipe written in the margin of a notebook. Three generations of women from the Central Valleys of Oaxaca perfected the masa — the same one we grind here, every morning, before the city wakes up.

Casa Maíz was born from a simple conviction: Dubai deserves real Mexican food. Not Tex-Mex. Not chain tacos. The kind that takes time, that smells of smoke, of lime, of history.

Our team is multicultural, our kitchen runs on wood fire, and our corn comes directly from indigenous farmers in Oaxaca and Guerrero. We nixtamalize it ourselves. We hand-press every tortilla. We don't cut corners — because shortcuts show.
