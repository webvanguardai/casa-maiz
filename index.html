Our Story

From Oaxaca
to Dubai.

It started with a comal, a bag of dried corn, and a recipe written in the margin of a notebook. Three generations of women from the Central Valleys of Oaxaca perfected the masa — the same masa we grind here, every morning, before the city wakes up.

Casa Maíz was born from a simple conviction: Dubai deserves real Mexican food. Not Tex-Mex. Not chain tacos. The kind that takes time, that smells like smoke and lime and history.

Our team is multicultural, our kitchen is wood-fired, and our corn is sourced directly from indigenous producers in Oaxaca and Guerrero. We nixtamalize it ourselves. We press every tortilla by hand. We don't take shortcuts — because you can taste shortcuts.
